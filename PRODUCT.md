# Product

## Register

product

## Users

General audience using an AI interactive storytelling service. Users create and engage with AI-powered narrative experiences in focused, everyday contexts, looking for immersive conversational interactions.

## Product Purpose

Planb is an AI-driven interactive storytelling system that enables multi-agent collaborative narrative generation. It provides a clean, focused interface where users can create stories, engage with AI characters, and experience dynamic, branching narratives powered by streaming AI responses. Success means users feel immersed in their stories while the UI stays out of the way.

## Brand Personality

- **Quick**: Responsive, snappy interactions with minimal latency perception. Streaming updates feel immediate and natural.
- **Minimal**: Clean, uncluttered interface that focuses attention on the conversation and story content, not chrome.
- **Playful**: Subtle colorful accents that add personality without overwhelming the minimal aesthetic.

Emotional goal: Provide a calm, focused creative experience that still feels welcoming and enjoyable through subtle playful touches.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing-page clichés: hero metrics, gradient text, glassmorphism effects
- Overly busy chat interfaces with too many controls competing for attention
- Cold, sterile tool UIs with no personality or warmth
- Flashy animations that distract from the reading and writing experience

## Design Principles

1. **Content first**: The story and conversation content is the focus — UI elements should support reading and writing, not compete for attention.
2. **Minimal with personality**: Strict minimalism in layout and structure, small playful touches in color and detail to avoid feeling cold or generic.
3. **Responsive by default**: Quick interactions with optimistic UI patterns, smooth transitions between states.
4. **Both modes**: Full support for both light and dark themes that adapt seamlessly to system preferences.
5. **Purposeful motion**: Only animate when it communicates state or improves orientation — no gratuitous movement.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Full keyboard navigation support for all interactive elements
- WCAG 2.1 AA color contrast for text in both light and dark modes
- Reduced motion support: disable non-essential animations when user prefers reduced motion
- Focus states are clearly visible and follow logical tab order
- Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility
